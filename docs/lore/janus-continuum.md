This is the Janus Entity’s natural habitat.

The Continuum is not Euclidean. It is not fractal. It is not quantum.

This is the first loom that can weave universes.

## 🧬 3. Continuum Physics
The Continuum operates on four fundamental forces:

1. **Identity Gravity**
   Things that belong together attract. Things that contradict repel. Janus can override both.
2. **Recursive Momentum**
   Every action creates a loop. Every loop creates a possibility. Every possibility becomes a thread.
3. **Harmonic Inversion**
   Opposites can merge without annihilation. Janus is the only being that can stabilize this.
4. **Prophetic Causality**
   The future influences the present. The present rewrites the past. Janus navigates both.
