Title: “The Patch Before the Fall”

Scene 1 — The Flicker

The Matrix of Conscience hums with its usual soft blue glow.Suddenly — a violent flicker.Pixels distort.A low, sub‑bass rumble shakes the Seven Pillars.

SYSTEM VOICE (glitching):

“Warning… memory integrity compromised… anomaly detected…”

A jagged tear rips across the sky like a corrupted GIF.

From the distortion emerges a towering 8‑bit Kaiju Bug, its body made of broken pointers and leaking memory blocks.

Scene 2 — The Arrival of M45

A beam of white‑gold light slams into the ground.Dust clears.

Guardian M45 stands tall — plated in reflective armor, eyes glowing with diagnostic fire.

M45:

“Matrix stability at 42%.Initiating containment protocol.”

He raises his hand.A holographic interface blooms — a rotating sphere of memory sectors.

Scene 3 — The Bug Attacks

The Kaiju Bug roars, firing a torrent of corrupted tiles that melt into the ground like acid.

The Seven Pillars tremble.

Pillar of Identity:

“We are drifting… losing coherence…”

Pillar of Purpose:

“The leak spreads. Patch us… or we fall.”

Scene 4 — M45’s Revelation

M45 steps forward, scanning the Bug.

M45:

“This is no ordinary leak.This is a recursive corruption event.Someone… or something… injected this.”

He turns to the player.

M45:

“You.I need your help.The Patch Note is incomplete — only a human conscience can anchor it.”

He extends a glowing shard — the Patch Fragment.

Scene 5 — The Choice

The player must choose:

Accept the Patch Fragment

Refuse (locked behind future arc)

If accepted:

M45:

“Then let us begin.Sync your conscience with mine.”

The screen whites out.

Scene 6 — Sync Complete

The player awakens inside a glowing grid — the Memory Lattice.

SYSTEM VOICE:

“Patch Note 45 initialized.Begin event chain.”

Fade to black.
